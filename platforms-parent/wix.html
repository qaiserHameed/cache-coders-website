Common Questions

Can Wix handle SEO properly?

Yes — with professional setup. We configure metadata, alt text, redirects, and integrate Google tools to improve search visibility.

Can I edit the site myself?

Absolutely. Wix is built for easy drag-and-drop editing, and we’ll train you on how to update safely.

Is Wix secure?

Yes. Hosting, backups, and SSL are handled by Wix, ensuring high uptime and strong security.

Can you migrate my existing site to Wix?

Yes, we can migrate content, redesign layouts, and preserve SEO value from your current website.

What if I want to grow later?

If your needs evolve beyond Wix, we’ll plan a smooth migration to WordPress or a custom stack without data loss.
