Why businesses choose Framer:

Common Questions

Can Framer handle SEO well?

Yes. Framer now supports metadata editing, alt text, and fast-loading sites — and we enhance it with structured data for search engines.

Is Framer suitable for large websites?

It’s perfect for creative sites, campaigns, and small-to-mid websites. For enterprise or content-heavy projects, we’ll recommend a hybrid setup.

Can you integrate forms and CRM tools?

Absolutely. We integrate forms with HubSpot, Mailchimp, or any tool you prefer using APIs or automation platforms.

Will my animations slow the site down?

No. We optimize motion using Framer Motion and efficient loading — so you get dynamic visuals with zero lag.

Do you provide templates or full custom designs?

Both. We can start from your brand guidelines or design something entirely new — all custom-built in Framer.
